Welcome

Welcome to my academic homepage. I am Dr. Dare Samuel, a lecturer in Human Anatomy with a passion for teaching and research. This site serves as a resource hub for students and colleagues.

About Me

A highly motivated and detail-oriented neuroanatomist/neuroscientist with a Bachelor and Master degrees in Human Anatomy and a PhD focused on neural behavioral neuroscience, molecular neuropharmacology, and circuit mechanisms. Well experienced in conducting integrated behavioral, histological, and molecular studies, with proficiency in NORT, OFT, ELISA, PCR, RT-PCR, and tissue-based assays. Fortified with strong foundation in linking behavior to underlying neural and biochemical processes supported by rigorous experimental design and data analysis skills. Demonstrated ability to manage complex research projects, communicate scientific findings effectively, and collaborate in multidisciplinary teams. I have contributed enormously to training medical doctors and paramedical professionals as well as in the development/review of anatomy curriculum in the course of my teaching/research career.
